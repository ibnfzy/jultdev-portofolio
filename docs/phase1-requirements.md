# Phase 1 Website Requirements

## Website Goal
The portfolio website will highlight Jul's professional identity as a full-stack developer, showcasing expertise in modern web technologies, problem-solving capability, and commitment to delivering user-centered solutions.

## Target Audience
- Potential clients seeking freelance development services.
- Recruiters and hiring managers evaluating technical candidates.
- Collaborators and peers interested in networking or joint projects.

## Key Features
- **Hero section** summarizing Jul's role, value proposition, and call-to-action buttons for hiring or viewing work.
- **About section** detailing background, experience highlights, and personal mission statement.
- **Skills section** organized by categories (frontend, backend, tooling) with proficiency indicators.
- **Featured projects** with case studies, technologies used, visuals, and links to live demos or repositories.
- **Services section** outlining offerings such as web app development, UI/UX implementation, and maintenance packages.
- **Testimonials** carousel to establish credibility.
- **Contact section** with form, direct email link, and social profiles.
- **Blog/news updates** area to share insights, tutorials, and announcements.
- Responsive layout with smooth navigation and accessibility best practices.

## Sitemap
- `/` – Homepage with hero, about, skills, projects, services, testimonials, blog highlights, and contact.
- `/projects` – Full portfolio of projects with filters and detailed case studies.
- `/services` – Detailed service descriptions, pricing tiers, and FAQs.
- `/about` – Expanded biography, timeline, and values.
- `/blog` – Articles, tutorials, and announcements.
- `/contact` – Dedicated contact form, availability schedule, and map (if applicable).

## Style Guidelines (Initial)
- **Color Palette:** Primary deep navy (#0A1F44), accent teal (#1ABC9C), warm highlight (#F1C40F), background light gray (#F7F9FC), neutral dark text (#1C1C1C).
- **Typography:** Pair "Poppins" for headings with "Inter" or "Roboto" for body text; ensure accessible font sizes and generous line spacing.
- **Imagery & Iconography:** Use clean, minimal illustrations or subtle geometric patterns; prefer custom project screenshots over stock imagery.
- **Tone & Voice:** Confident, approachable, and solution-oriented with emphasis on collaboration and measurable results.
